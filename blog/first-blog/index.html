First Blog And Some Mind Garbage

[ 2022-06-07 ]


OK finally i'm setting up my website. I always wanted to do it, i was always looking forward to having a website, but never bothered about it because i did not had any urgent need for having a full fleged website.

Anyways, here we are...you are reading this blog on my website. A website where i can share my thoughts and ideas without any restrictions, Or thats what i think about it. Now a days i feel like things are not in our hands anymore or they never have been.

Independent...Selfsufficient....Selfreliant we all praise for these feelings. Feeling of being independent. Not being able to controlled by anyone. But are they really true. Can this be actually achieved.

The straight answer is NO.

If some of you guys are hurt by that you can leave. I can understand you are trying to protect your inner soul that has a solidified image of yourself that you wanna be and become and you are trying your best to not get it liquify by the bitter acidic truth.

The thing is ... we are veryy protective and protected. We wanna stay in the bubble we blew. But why can't we. It's comfy and warm.

But trust me on this, letting your inner image get little softter to create a room for more knowledge and things you never been considering important, will change your ability to think to the extent you could only experience but never imagine.

We as humans, are always under someone's or something's influence. Influence of politics, Influence of community, Influence of group. If you actually sit and note down things you are being influenced by would fill up your pages but won't stop occuring in your mind.

We are living in a new era hard time. Coz truly throughout history things have never never never been easy good smooth and calm.

As a individual you can do nothing(big).

If you wanna do something big something that made sense to actually achieve. something that changed created a new path for more people to walk on to. something that gave you inner peace to do it. You can't do it alone. You have to become people person. There is no choice.

You can be a introvert all your life, sit in a dark room with a computer and have everying happning on this planet on your screen. and no one would stop you. And no one should if thats what gives you inner peace.

Collaboration is a vital tool to achieve something big, big in as beyound imagination. Without collaboration humans are apes, that would die soon coz apes do collaborate.

People comming together makes difference. People acting together makes difference. People thinking together makes difference.

and about the free-speech, you just need to deal with it.

Peace!
