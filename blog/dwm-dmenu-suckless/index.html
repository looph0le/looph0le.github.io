What are Suckless Programs ?

> Suckless programs are highly based on the unix philosophy

A program must do only one thing, and one thing perfectly

There is no doubt that in today's world, software has gone more advance than they ever have been. But how you define advance.

Maybe by the

  • > Number Of Features
  • > Speed Of Execution
  • > Ease Of Use

Software is built surrounding these limitations. And at the end most of the softwares are unable to fullfill one or even two above 'definings'. That is where suckless programs catch attention, They are just perfect from what we talked about earlier.

They are
  • > Minimal
  • > Extreamly Fast [Fully writen in C]
  • > Easy to configure [If you learn some C]
